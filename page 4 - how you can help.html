How You Can Help

Be An Ally:

  • Listen without judging when someone shares their experiences.
  • Respect people's names, pronouns, and identities.
  • Speak up when you hear unfair or hurtful comments.
  • Offer support in ways that make people feel safe and included.
  • Learn more about LGBTQIA+ issues instead of relying on stereotypes.
  • Small acts of kindness and acceptance can make a big difference.

Raise Awareness:

  • Share reliable information about LGBTQIA+ youth homelessness.
  • Help others understand how family rejection and discrimination can affect safety.
  • Encourage respectful conversations at school and in the community.
  • Use social media responsibly to spread awareness, not misinformation.
  • Support events, campaigns, or school projects that promote inclusion.
  • Awareness can help reduce stigma and connect more young people to support.

Donate:

You can support organisations that provide help, housing, advocacy, or crisis support for vulnerable LGBTQIA+ youth.
